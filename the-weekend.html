About The Weeknd

The Weeknd, known as Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer.

He rose to fame with his unconventional music production, artistic reinventions, and falsetto vocals.

The Weeknd has received numerous awards and accolades for his work, including Grammy Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and Juno Awards
