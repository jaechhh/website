About SELENA GOMEZ

Early Life: Selena Marie Gomez was born on July 22, 1992, in Grand Prairie, Texas, USA. She began her career in the entertainment industry at a young age, appearing on the children's television series "Barney & Friends."

Acting Career: Gomez gained widespread recognition for her role as Alex Russo on the Disney Channel series "Wizards of Waverly Place," which aired from 2007 to 2012. She also acted in various films and television shows during her early career.

Music Career: Selena Gomez is a successful pop singer and songwriter. She formed the band Selena Gomez & the Scene and released albums such as "Kiss & Tell" (2009), "A Year Without Rain" (2010), and "When the Sun Goes Down" (2011).

Fashion and Beauty: Selena Gomez has also made her mark in the fashion and beauty industries. She launched her makeup line called "Rare Beauty" in 2020, which focuses on promoting self-expression and self-acceptance

Continued Success: As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, Selena Gomez remained a prominent and influential figure in the entertainment industry. She has a large and dedicated fan base and continues to release music, act in film and television, and engage in philanthropic endeavors.
