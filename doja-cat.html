About DOJA CAT

Early Life: Doja Cat was born on October 21, 1995, in Los Angeles, California. She comes from a family with a background in the arts, as her South African mother is a painter, and her Jewish-American father is a composer.

Musical Career: She began making music and releasing songs on platforms like SoundCloud as a teenager. Her early work combined elements of hip-hop, R&B, and electronic music.

Breakthrough: Doja Cat's breakthrough came with the release of her single "Mooo!" in 2018, which went viral on social media due to its quirky and humorous music video. This helped her gain significant online attention.

"Say So" and Album Success: In 2019, Doja Cat released the song "Say So" as part of her second studio album, "Hot Pink." The song became a massive hit, reaching the top of the Billboard Hot 100 chart. "Hot Pink" received critical acclaim and established her as a mainstream artist.

Personal Life: While Doja Cat is known for her music and public persona, she tends to keep her personal life relatively private.
